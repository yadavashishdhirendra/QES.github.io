From One Hour to Three Minutes:
Dramatically Accelerating N-PORT Filing

Challenge

A global investment management firm wanted to lessen the complexity and cost of complying with the SEC’s Modernization Reporting rules. The company needed a partner to aggregate vast amounts of data from multiple sources on an accelerated timeline — and keep all the work on premises.

Solution

QES analyzed the client’s data and systems and designed a custom, on-site, cloud-based technology solution that was efficient, accurate and blazing fast.

Result

The time to process the data with N-PORT was dramatically reduced from about an hour to just three minutes. With the custom technology now in place, the client was able to meet its ongoing monthly and annual reporting deadlines quickly and easily—and with no additional investment.
